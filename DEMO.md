## Recording Tips

- **Pace**: Speak clearly but briskly—3 minutes is tight
- **Rehearse**: Run through 2-3 times to nail timing
- **Screen**: 1920x1080, hide desktop clutter, fullscreen browser
- **Cursor**: Use a cursor highlighter tool
- **Audio**: Use a decent mic, minimize background noise
- **Cuts**: Quick cuts keep energy high
- **Demo Mode**: Pre-test to ensure it loads instantly
